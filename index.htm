FLP

Are you looking to make a remix or learn more about music production? Then project files are what you need! You can download project files including full songs and make remixes or download templates to learn more about structure and the basics of creating a track.

Sample

Do you need loops or samples? We have lots of sample packs including high quality samples for a big variety of genres. The samples are named with the correct BPM and key to make it easier for you to pick the right ones for your track.

Acapella

Do you need vocals for your new track? Check out our high quality acapellas! The acapellas are named with the correct BPM and key to make it easier for you to pick the right one.

MIDI

Do you need a chord progression or a melody to get started on a new track or remix? We have both original and well known MIDI-files for you to download.
